Privacy Policy

We keep the policy readable and practical: what we collect, what we do not collect, how we use it, and how you stay in control.

1. Introduction

FOMO NOMO LLC respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this policy. The app is designed to host user-generated content and facilitate public meetups.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, login, and to verify your affiliation with a specific school or institution (e.g., a .edu domain) in order to assign you to the correct campus community/feed.
  • User-Generated Content: Feed posts, comments, likes, and event details.
  • Photos and Camera Roll: Access only with permission for profile pictures and event cover photos.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No Location Tracking: We do not collect, track, or store GPS or device location data.
  • No Bluetooth Data: Bluetooth permissions are not requested or used.
  • No Direct Messaging: The platform does not support or store 1-on-1 private messaging.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of the application.
  • Display your profile, event details, and posts to other users.
  • Manage your account and provide customer support.
  • Verify institutional email domains to determine feed access and restrict cross-campus visibility.

5. Content Moderation and Safety

We use automated keyword/pattern filtering on posts and comments, combined with user-submitted reports and human review. Reported content is reviewed on an ongoing basis (typically within 24 hours) and, where it violates our Community Guidelines, removed. Users may block other users and report individual posts or comments.

6. Sharing Your Information

Posts, comments, and event content you create are visible to other users. We do not sell your personal information and only disclose it when required by law or valid public authority requests.

7. Your Choices

  • Camera Access: Revoke camera roll access through your device settings.
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated content by contacting us.
  • Blocking: You may block other users at any time, which prevents them from viewing your content or contacting you within the app.

8. Institutional Email Verification

Feeds are strictly segmented by verified school email domains. Users without a matching, verified domain cannot access or view that specific campus's feed.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices can be sent to FOMO NOMO LLC at support@fomonomoapp.com.
