Trust & Safety

FOMO NOMO only works if it feels safe to show up. Here's how we keep it that way:

Verified campus communities: Every feed is tied to a verified school email (like @ucf.edu). You'll only ever see your own campus's events and posts. No outside accounts or cross-campus noise.

Report anything, instantly: Every post, comment, and profile has a Report option built right in. If something's off, flag it and our team reviews it.

Block whoever you want: Blocking is one tap and immediate. A blocked user can't see your content, comment on your posts, or contact you.

We actually moderate: Reports don't go into a void. Content that breaks our Community Guidelines gets removed, and repeat or serious violations lead to suspension or a permanent ban.

Meeting up IRL? A few basics: meet in public, well-lit places, tell a friend where you're headed, and trust your gut. FOMO NOMO helps you find what's happening - the same common sense you'd use meeting anyone new still applies.

Something feel wrong? Reach us anytime at support@fomonomoapp.com.
