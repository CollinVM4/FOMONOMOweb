Support

We’re here to help with account issues, technical support, and general questions. Below are the most common answers.

Contact Us

For account issues, technical support, or general inquiries, contact FOMO NOMO LLC at support@fomonomoapp.com.

Please allow 24 to 48 hours for a response.

Organizer Pro Billing

All subscriptions, including Organizer Pro, are managed through your app store account settings.

  1. Open your app store account settings.
  2. Select Subscriptions.
  3. Select FOMO NOMO to cancel or manage billing.

Reporting Content

If you see a post that violates our Community Guidelines, tap the options menu next to the post, then choose Report or Block.

Our moderation team reviews flagged content and takes appropriate action.

Why Was My Post Removed?

Posts are removed when they violate the Community Guidelines. The platform is designed around upcoming meetups, so self-promotion, advertisements, and unrelated content may be removed to keep the feed focused.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time. Email support@fomonomoapp.com from the address associated with your account, and we’ll process the request.
